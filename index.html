Marketplace · Startup · B2B Unscheduled
El Ra7ba — B2B Feed & Livestock Marketplace
Digital marketplace for animal feed (3laf) trading — replaces scam-prone Facebook groups with verified profiles and real-time pricing.
TBD
0%

There is no specialized digital platform for the feed/livestock trade in Tunisia today — everything runs through Facebook groups and phone calls with brokers. Those groups are full of scams, have no price tracking, and listings disappear from the feed in hours. A platform with verified profiles, real-time regional pricing grids, and advanced filtering could become the standard for the sector — the gap is wide open.
